Our Story

Every Flavor Tells a Story

Nawabi Lazzat specializes in authentic North Indian and Mughlai cuisine, offering a royal dining experience with traditional flavors. Located in Kharar, Mohali, we serve both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes crafted with time-honored recipes. Our family-friendly and welcoming atmosphere makes us the perfect choice for casual dining or celebrations. We offer dine-in, takeout, delivery through Swiggy and Zomato, and catering services.
